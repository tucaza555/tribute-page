Arnold Schwarzenegger in his prime

Here's a time line of Arnold Schwarzenegger life:

  • 1947 - Born in a small village bordering the Styrian capital Graz, in Austria
  • 1962 - Arnold Began his body building career he was 15 years and he was discovored and helped by the canadian bodybuilder Joe Wieder.
  • 1968 - Moved to California to train and compete in bigger events in the United States
  • 1970 - He won four more Mr. Universe titles and then the professional Mr. Olympia title six years in a row before retiring.
  • 1976 - After acting in a few small parts, Schwarzenegger received a Golden Globe Award for Best Newcomer for his performance in Stay Hungry (1976).
  • 1981 - Arnold went to University and graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Superior with a degree in Business and International Economics
  • 2003 - In the recall election for Governor of California, Schwarzenegger placed his candidacy.
